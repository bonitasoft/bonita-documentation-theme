# Typographic Rules Of Use

Each typeface is assigned to a type of content, follows a hierarchy and well-defined rules of use.

## Rules

| Type                              | Font                                    | Line Height | Letter Spacing |
|-----------------------------------|-----------------------------------------|-------------|----------------|
| Title larger than 48px            | PP Neue Montreal, Medium                | 130%        | -1%            |
| Title equal to/less than 32px     | PP Neue Montreal, Medium                | 130%        | -1%            |
| Content equal to or less than 20px| PP Neue Montreal, Medium                | 150%        | 0%             |
| Link equal to/less than 18px      | PP Neue Montreal, Medium                | 120%        | -1%            |
| Tags/labels                       | Faculty Glyphic, Regular, Capitals only | 140%        | -2%            |

## Notes

- **Default body weight** is Medium (`font-weight: 500`).
- **Headings** (h1–h3) use Medium with tight line-height (1.3) and slightly negative letter-spacing (-0.01em).
- **Faculty Glyphic** font files are not yet provided. Tags/labels will fall back to the body font until the font is added.
